About Salon Talks

Salon Talks is an online space for meaningful conversations, designed to connect people who may feel lonely and isolated. Our events provide a relaxed, welcoming environment where you can share your thoughts, stories, and ideas with others who share similar interests. From discussing current events to reflecting on life’s experiences, or sharing your love for books, food, or nature, each Salon Talk brings people together for genuine connection. Whether you love to chat or prefer to listen, you’ll find a community that values what you have to say.

Why Choose Salon Talks?

We know that staying connected can be difficult, especially as we age. Salon Talks was created to combat loneliness and foster meaningful interactions. All of our events are held online, so you can participate from the comfort of home, without worrying about technology—we’re here to guide you every step of the way.

How it works

It’s simple! Browse our upcoming events, sign up, and join the conversation online. Each event has a specific theme, such as Life Conversations, Discussing the Present, or Food & Drink, and we encourage you to share your experiences with others. No technical skills are required, just a willingness to engage in a fun, supportive environment.

Join Us

Salon Talks is a place to connect, reflect, and enjoy meaningful conversations. If you’re looking for a way to meet new people and engage in inspiring discussions, sign up for one of our upcoming events and join the conversation.
